Questions
What happens when someone calls?

Jollyphone picks up on the first ring, greets the caller in your business's voice, and does the thing they called for — books the table, schedules the cleaning, takes the intake. Anything it can't finish, it hands to you with a summary.

Does it sound like a robot?

No. Most callers never notice. The ones who ask are told the truth, politely, and keep talking anyway.

Which industries does it work for?

All of them. Restaurants, dental offices, law firms, salons, clinics, contractors — if your phone rings, Jollyphone answers it. The agent is trained on your business, not a template.

What if it can't answer a question?

It says so — then takes a message, offers a callback, or transfers to a human, whichever you've chosen. It never improvises an answer it doesn't have.

How do I get started?

Press the button in the orb. Jollyphone calls you, and the demo is the onboarding.
